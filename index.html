Q2 2025 Summary

  • Achieved net sales of $1.3 billion and adjusted EBITDA of $336 million in Q2 2025, with sequential improvements driven by better fixed cost absorption and cost savings.
  • Energy Storage segment saw 15% volume growth year-over-year, with Q2 2025 sales volumes at 58 kT LCE, supported by record production from the integrated conversion network.
  • Reduced full-year 2025 capital expenditure outlook to $650-700 million, down approximately 60% year-over-year, enhancing financial flexibility.
  • Expect to achieve positive free cash flow in FY 2025, assuming current lithium market pricing persists, with operating cash flow conversion targeted at >80%.
  • Global lithium demand remains strong, led by 35% year-over-year growth in EV sales through May 2025, particularly in China, Europe, and North America.

Q1 2025 Summary

  • Achieved net sales of $1.1 billion and adjusted EBITDA of $267 million in Q1 2025, with year-over-year gains in Specialties (+30%) and Ketjen (+76%).
  • Cash from operations was $545 million, with operating cash flow conversion at 204% (73% excluding customer prepayment), and net debt to adjusted EBITDA at 2.4x, well below the covenant limit.
  • Energy Storage segment reported Q1 2025 sales volumes of 44 kT LCE, with volume growth expected between 0% and 10% year-over-year, and ~50% of 2025 E salts volumes sold on long-term agreements.
  • Through April, achieved ~90% run-rate against the midpoint $350 million cost and productivity improvement target, identifying opportunities to reach the high end of $300M to $400M range.
  • Maintaining FY 2025 outlook considerations, with line of sight to breakeven free cash flow, and introducing an updated lithium supply-demand outlook expecting 2025 to be more balanced.

Q4 2024 Summary

  • Achieved Q4 2024 net sales of $1,232 million and adjusted EBITDA of $251 million, with year-over-year improvements in all business segments, driven by higher volumes and productivity gains.
  • FY 2024 adjusted EBITDA of $1,140 million was in line with outlook, supported by cost improvements, higher volumes, and contract performance, despite a 44% decline in net sales year-over-year.
  • Energy Storage segment saw a 26% year-over-year increase in LCE sales volumes, reaching 203 kt for FY 2024, with expectations of 0% to 10% volume growth in FY 2025.
  • Introduced FY 2025 outlook with net sales projected at $4.9B-$7.0B and adjusted EBITDA at $0.8B-$2.7B, aiming for breakeven free cash flow, supported by reduced capital expenditures of $700M-$800M (down >50% YoY).
  • Executed cost and productivity actions, achieving over 50% run-rate of $300M-$400M target by year-end 2024, with plans to reach 100% run-rate by YE 2025, enhancing cash conversion expected to exceed 80% in FY 2025.

Q3 2024 Summary

  • Q3 2024 net sales were $1,355 million, down 41% year-over-year, with volume growth in Energy Storage (+16%) and Specialties (+4%).
  • Adjusted EBITDA was $211 million, a 68% decrease year-over-year, with improvements in Specialties (+22%) and Ketjen (+134%).
  • Operating cash flow was $241 million, achieving cash conversion of >100%, with FY 2024E cash conversion expected at ~50%.
  • The company is implementing cost and productivity improvements targeting $300-$400 million annually, reducing the global workforce by 6-7% (or ~15% of non-manufacturing workforce).
  • FY 2024 outlook considerations maintained, with net sales expected at the lower end and adjusted EBITDA towards the middle of the $12-$15/kg lithium market price scenario range.
